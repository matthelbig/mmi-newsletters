5 Reasons your new pool will make you everyone’s best friend this summer.



So you made the decision to own one of the best pools and oases in town. You’re enjoying it. How could you not? Maybe it’s an HGTV certified Posh Plunge Pool like 9 of ours happen to be. Maybe it’s so spectacular you’re keeping it all to yourself. Or maybe you’re just looking for the best ideas to host the perfect poolside get-together. Here is our list of the top 5 reasons your new pool will make you everyone’s best friend this summer.

Memorial Day, 4th of July, & Labor Day

The three wise men of summer. Nothing celebrates our country quite as well as a hot grill next to a relaxing pool. Now you'll be able to mark the beginning, middle, and end of summer with a poolside grillathon.

The Platinum Pools team celebrates with an icy beverage and a cannonball contest. Post your own poolside pics to our Facebook page to show us how you’re enjoying your Aquatech pool!

Your Very Own Poolside Bar

Because alcohol isn't allowed at the public pool, and that’s a crying shame. When you have your own pool, you get to set the rules. And while running and glass containers are probably still not a great idea, a cold drink with a tiny umbrella seems like the best idea.
  • Cucumber-Lemonade Chiller
  • Tequila Sunrise Margarita
  • Red Sangria
  • Make your own Watermelon Keg!
Check out our favorite summertime drinks so you can relax in style!

Suburban Staycation

So you chose to turn your backyard into a memorial of your favorite summer cottage from your childhood. Or was it an ode to that exotic vacation in the Bahamas?

All we know is you brought your favorite vacation home with you and you’re better off for it. Now you can call your best friends and invite them on the vacation they’ve been waiting for-ETA: 15 minutes.

Your new exercise routine just became pro-status

Just search the internet for the benefits of swimming or working out in water. There is a seemingly endless supply of exercises made more efficient by doing them in your pool.

Host your friends for a water yoga session. The added resistance of the water helps burn calories and allows you to push your limits. If an amazing beach body is your goal, your friends will be thanking you all the way to the beach.

Because you know that a company who makes your backyard look this good, knows how to take care of it too.

We have the play-by-play of pool maintenance broken out for you- what you can do yourselves, what you can trust us to handle. So even when your midnight-swim ends in a hiccup, you know you can make a quick phone call for some help.

We’ll also handle getting your pool ready for the yearly opening ceremony, and close it down for you when you finally yield to the oncoming polar vortex season. That’s right, form the start of the season to the frigid end, our fleet of certified technicians have you covered.
