This Holiday Season Drive with Confidence
 
During harsh winter months you need balance and an even distribution of power for maximum traction, instead of the slip-then-grip alternative.

Most Subarus’ come standard with Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive for better stability, efficiency and a quicker response to slipper conditions. It’s always nice to have an extra set of eyes on the road and an extra foot on the brake when you drive. Which is exactly what you get with EyeSight driver assist technology.*
  • When you’re on the road, the Adaptive Cruise Control safely maintains the speed you set.
  • Lane Departure & Sway Warning is designed to protect you when that happens.
  • Pre-Collision Braking helps you avoid or reduce frontal impact by alerting you and applying full braking forces in emergency situations.

When equipped with Subaru EyeSight, the 2014 Outback and Legacy models received the highest possible score in front crash prevention by the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety, higher than both the Honda Accord and Ford Fushion.
